ABOUT US


For over ten years Hobo Seafood has provided the highest quality "wild caught" shrimp to their customers.  Owned and operated by Lee and Madge Williams, Hobo Seafood is a family company that embodies the American entrepreneurial spirit.

We started out with just one shrimp trawler and today it's fifteen trawlers and a distribution site.  In addition, Hobo Seafood employs nearly 100 people in rural Hyde County, NC.
